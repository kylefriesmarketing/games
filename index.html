THE SHELF

Branching stories, a toybox war, and Dumb Tony's corner of the shelf. Everything here runs in your browser — no installs, no accounts, nothing but choices and their consequences.

SOUTH
polar epic · antarctica 1915 · you are the boss
The ship is crushed, the ice is drifting, and twenty-seven men are reading your face to learn whether they live. The only true ending is all of them.
STILL BREATHING
survival thriller · six true ordeals
A plane down in the white. A raft on the whole Atlantic. A boulder, a lost trail, a cut rope, a desert. Every deadly choice is a real survival myth — grounded in the ones who came back.
NINE CIRCLES
a descent · after Dante
Walk down through Hell with a dead poet. Carry three names. Keep the star lit. Feel it, and walk on.
CHOOSE WISELY
branching storybook · the shop remembers
A corner shop where every gift takes something back. Fifty-six endings. The shop remembers you between visits — and it lies at high haunt.
NOBODY
the Odyssey · a homecoming · twenty years
You are Odysseus. Your name is a resource that can go dark, the sea keeps a twenty-year grudge, and Penelope's loom is the clock. The poem says he came home alone, too late. Argue with the poem.
ELEMENTARY
deduction · Sherlock Holmes · wrong is a branch
You are Sherlock Holmes. Notice the tell, draw the red thread across the Index, name the guilty. No one greys out the false clues — believe one and an innocent hangs, and Watson whispers a word in your ear: Norbury.
CURIOUSER
dream & nonsense · Alice in Wonderland
You're the wrong size for every room, every creature answers with a worse question, and being reasonable is the trap. You can't win a dream — you can only wake as yourself.
DRACULA
ensemble gothic horror · Bram Stoker · the collective hunt
You are not one hero but a band, each keeping a diary. Pool your scattered papers into one Dossier until the pattern shows — and once he has touched Mina, he reads it over your shoulder. The book loses Lucy and Quincey. Argue with the book.
AGE OF TOYS
real-time strategy · the toybox war
Nine maps, plastic armies, and a bathtub naval theatre. The floor is the front line.
BRAINROT: RISE OF THE MEME
strategy · by dumb tony
Infect humanity with terminal brainrot before the world cures itself. Ride Trend Heat, spend Virality, outrun The Cure.
CHAMELEON 3D
kitchen crawl · by dumb tony
You are small, sticky-footed, and the kitchen is enormous. Blend in, climb everything, don't get caught.
TIDEBOUND
survival visual novel · by dumb tony
Your plane goes down beside an island no chart admits exists. Earn the trust of one wild animal. The island's Ledger forgets nothing.
